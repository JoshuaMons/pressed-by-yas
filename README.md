# Pressed by Yas — Nail Technician Website

A stylish, modern single-page website for [@pressedbyyas](https://www.instagram.com/pressedbyyas/). Elegant design with warm cream, terracotta, and sage accents. Tailored for custom press-on nail sets.

## Quick Start

1. Open `index.html` in your browser, or
2. Use a simple local server (e.g. `npx serve .`) for the best experience

## Customize for Your Business

Edit `index.html` to personalize:

- **Contact info**: Add your phone and email from your Instagram bio (if you share them)
- **Location**: Add your city/region if you offer local pickup
- **About section**: Update the bio and stats to match your experience
- **Gallery**: Add photos from your [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/pressedbyyas/) — save images from your posts and replace the placeholder divs with `<img src="images/your-photo.jpg" alt="Description">`

## Adding Your Photos

For the gallery and about section, replace the placeholder divs with:

```html
<img src="images/your-nail.jpg" alt="Description of the design">
```

Create an `images` folder and add your photos there.

## File Structure

nail-tech-website/

├── styles.css    # All styling
├── script.js     # Mobile menu & scroll effects

## Optional: Deploy Online

To put this online:

- **Netlify** or **Vercel**: Drag and drop the folder; free hosting
- **GitHub Pages**: Push to a repo and enable Pages
- **Your own hosting**: Upload the folder via FTP

No build step required—just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
