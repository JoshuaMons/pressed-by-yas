Before You Book

Policy & House Rules

House Rules

  • Come with clean nails! No nail polish or old gel from different artists. No dirt lines.
  • I do not work on sets from others.
  • Don't bring a guest, unless we've discussed this.
  • I don't do simple sets! Don't book with me if you just want French tips etc.

Payments

  • All payments are preferably done by card, but cash is possible too. When paying in cash, please pay the exact amount.
  • NO refunds will be given after the appointment is finished!

Extra Info

  • If your nails are too damaged, I won't be able to work on them. DM me beforehand if you're unsure.
  • Soak offs can take up to 45 minutes. Keep this in mind while booking and always mention it when booking.
  • Keep in mind I'm not a magician!! I might not be able to exactly copy the design you want.
